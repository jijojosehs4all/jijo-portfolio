About Me

I'm a Product Manager and Full Stack Developer passionate about turning ideas into impactful digital products. My interest in coding began in my teens and evolved through college, where I discovered my strength in product ownership and management. Over the past four years, I've led Agile teams, delivered end-to-end solutions, and launched projects like Man Hour Report, a productivity platform for the electrical construction industry.

Eager to deepen my technical skills, I pursued PERN stack development through self-learning and the CNM Ingenuity Bootcamp. My goal is to grow at the intersection of Product Management, Design, and Web Development, creating user-focused products that drive real results.
