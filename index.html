About Me

I'm a Product Manager and Full Stack Developer(front-end focused) passionate about turning ideas into impactful digital products. My interest in software development began in my late teens and evolved through college, where I discovered my strength in product ownership and management. I’ve been active in the software development field since 2018, spending three years as a Product Owner/Manager where I worked closely with Agile teams and guided end-to-end product delivery. After a three-year break, I returned by taking on freelance web development projects and deepening my skills in frontend technologies. That experience sparked a stronger interest in full-stack development and ultimately led me to explore backend development more seriously.

Eager to deepen my technical skills, I pursued PERN stack development through self-learning and the CNM Ingenuity Bootcamp. My goal is to grow at the intersection of Product Management, Design, and Web Development, creating user-focused products that drive real results.
