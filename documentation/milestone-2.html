Content Strategy

Homepage/Hero Section

The homepage will introduce me with my full name(eg;Hi,I'm Jijo), a short description of who I am (e.g., Full Stack Developer), and call-to-action buttons that link to my projects and contact page. Social media links will also be displayed here,and with a cool background image.

About me

This section will contain a short biography, a professional photo, and key highlights of my background, skills, and career goals. It will give visitors a sense of my personality and professional journey. Directly below the introduction, three cards will highlight my educational accomplishments: a Bachelor's degree, my coding bootcamp experience, and a professional certification.

Skills

This section will serve as a comprehensive showcase of my technical capabilities, organized into clearly defined categories. The layout features a hero area with a title and description, followed by two main components: a proficiency chart and categorized technology lists.

Projects

This section will highlight my best work through a curated portfolio of three featured projects. The layout emphasizes visual appeal with prominent project images, clear descriptions, and easy access to both live demos and source code. This section serves as concrete proof of my technical abilities and problem-solving approach.

Experience

The Experience section will highlight my three most recent job roles in a clean, structured format. Each entry will include the company name, location, job title, and duration of employment. Below this, a concise job description will summarize my responsibilities and achievements for that role. To showcase my technical expertise, each job will list up to six key technologies and tools I used, such as programming languages, frameworks, databases, or project management tools.

Contact

The contact section will feature a simple form for users to reach me directly by email, along with links to my phone number and location.

Footer

The footer will contain footer copyright and social icons for consistent navigation across the site.
